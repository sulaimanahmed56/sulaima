Our Restaurant

Le Gourmet's Haven - A Culinary Delight at Le Pas Vrai Hotel

At Le Pas Vrai Hotel, we take immense pride in offering a diverse culinary experience that takes our guests on a delightful gastronomic journey around the world. Our four distinctive restaurants, each representing the rich and vibrant flavors of their respective cultures, promise to transport you to Italy, Mexico, Oman, and India without leaving the comfort of our luxurious hotel.

Italian Kitchen:Italian Trattoria - Bella Tavola

LIndulge in the heartwarming flavors of Italy at Bella Tavola, our authentic Italian trattoria. Our skilled Italian chefs craft dishes that pay homage to traditional recipes passed down through generations. From classic pasta dishes adorned with velvety sauces to wood-fired pizzas with a crispy crust and generous toppings, every bite is a celebration of rustic Italian charm. Pair your meal with a fine Italian wine from our curated selection for an unforgettable culinary experience.

Mexican Kitchen:Mexican Cantina - Sabores de México

At Sabores de México, the vibrant and lively spirit of Mexico comes alive. Our Mexican cantina offers an array of bold and flavorful dishes that showcase the true essence of this captivating cuisine. Savor the rich spices of traditional tacos, the comforting warmth of hearty enchiladas, and the zesty freshness of guacamole made from ripe avocados. Complete your experience with a refreshing margarita or a selection of premium tequilas to toast to the flavors of Mexico.

Omani Kitchen:Omani Oasis - Sahari Al Watan

Immerse yourself in the rich culinary heritage of Oman at Sahari Al Watan. Drawing inspiration from the country's coastal and desert influences, our Omani restaurant presents a symphony of aromatic spices and delightful textures. From succulent grilled meats marinated in traditional spices to fragrant rice dishes and fresh seafood caught from the Arabian Sea, every dish reflects the heart and soul of Oman. Experience the true hospitality of the region as you feast on Omani delicacies in an inviting and elegant setting.

Indian Kitchen: Indian Spice Trail - Aromas of India

Embark on a flavorful journey to the land of spices at Aromas of India. Our Indian restaurant captures the diversity of India's regional cuisines, bringing together an array of tantalizing flavors that are sure to please every palate. Delight in the subtle notes of North Indian curries, the tangy sensations of South Indian dosas and chutneys, and the aromatic blend of spices in classic biryanis. Experience the vibrancy of Indian culture through its food, as we present a menu that honors the rich traditions and modern influences of this captivating country.
